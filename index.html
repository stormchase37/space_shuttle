30 years of flight

Three of the Space Shuttles were involved in the construction of the International Space Station. It took 13 years to build the space station from 1998 to 2011. The space station provides a lab where astronauts can do experiments and study the effects of long term space flight on the human body. These studies will help us understand the effects on the human body on missions to Mars and back to the moon for an extended period of time. As the astronauts stay on the space station for up to six months at a time they must excercise because if they don't excercise they will lose body mass and muscle. The space station is longer than a football field. Without the space shuttle there would be no space station and therefore no space program after the space shuttle to take us beyond low earth orbit.
